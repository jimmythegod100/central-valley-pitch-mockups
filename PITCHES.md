## Universal objection handlers

| They say | You say |
|---|---|
| “Too expensive” | “It’s a fixed Launch Package — **[price]** — not hourly. Half now, half at launch. No monthly lock-in unless you want the $29 care plan.” |
| “I’ll think about it” | “Totally. I’ll text the sample today. Price holds through Friday if you want it this month.” |
| “My nephew can do it” | “Great — the mock is free to use as a reference. If it stalls, I’m here at the same price.” |
| “We get enough word of mouth” | “Keep word of mouth. The site catches strangers already searching — you’re leaving those on the table.” |
| “Can you do it cheaper?” | “I can trim to **[floor]** if we keep one page and you send photos this week. Below that I can’t do the form + launch properly.” |

## Package (say out loud)
1. One-page custom site from the mock  
2. Mobile-friendly + click-to-call  
3. Contact / quote / appointment form  
4. Basic local SEO + map  
5. One revision round + 30-day bug fixes  
6. Optional: Google Business Profile **$149** · Care **$29/mo**
