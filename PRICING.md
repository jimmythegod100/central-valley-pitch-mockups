2026 freelance one-pagers typically land **$800–$2,200** direct; median ~**$800–$1,500**. These quotes sit in that band, slightly friendly so you can **close first paid clients** and still look professional.

## Why each price (so you can defend it)

### 1. Margo's Logos — **$1,197**
- Broken live domain = urgent, high-trust pitch  
- Includes reconnect/replace site, quote form, service blocks, gallery slots  
- Slightly above a plain brochure because you’re fixing a public failure  

**If they push:** “I can do **$997** if we keep it to one page and you supply 4–6 photos this week.” Floor: **$897**.

### 2. Sole Saver — **$897**
- Simplest scope: hours, services, map, call button  
- Heritage shop; don’t oversell complexity  

**If they push:** **$797** with no photo shoot help. Floor: **$697** (only if cash same day).

### 3. Diego's Landscaping — **$997**
- Mobile-first + estimate form (lead capture)  
- Home-service sites pay for themselves on 1–2 jobs  

**If they push:** **$897**. Floor: **$797**.

### 4–5. Bruce's Handyman / Plumbing
- Alone: **$897** handyman · **$997** plumbing (license/trust page)  
- **Bundle: $1,497** (saves them ~$400 vs $897+$997)  

**Script:** “Two separate sites would be about $1,900. One brand with both services is **$1,497**.”

### 6. The Time Traveler — **$1,097**
- Appointment form + specialty copy (Howard Miller, senior discount, clock types)  
- Higher intent leads than a lawn quote form  

**If they push:** **$997**. Floor: **$897**.

## What’s included in every package

1. One custom mobile-friendly page (based on the mock they already saw)  
2. Click-to-call button  
3. Contact / quote / appointment form → their email or SMS  
4. Basic on-page SEO (title, headings, city + service keywords)  
5. Google Maps embed (where address exists)  
6. One revision round after first draft  
7. Launch help (connect domain or new `.com`)  
8. 30 days of bug-fix after launch  

**Not included (sell as add-ons):**
| Add-on | Price |
|---|---:|
| Google Business Profile setup / cleanup | **$149** |
| Extra revision rounds (each) | **$75** |
| Rush delivery (under 5 days) | **+25%** |
| Monthly care (hosting + small edits) | **$29/mo** or **$249/yr** |
| Extra page (About, Gallery, FAQ) | **$197** each |
| Logo refresh (simple) | **$249** |

## Payment terms (use every time)

- **50% to start**, **50% on launch** (before you hand over the domain/DNS)  
- Venmo / Zelle / cash / invoice  
- Work starts after deposit  
- If they ghost after draft: deposit is non-refundable; final due only if they want launch files  

---

## How to say the price without flinching

> “The Launch Package for what I showed you is **[price]**. That includes the page, the form, mobile, and getting it live — about **[days]** once I have your photos and deposit. Half to start, half when we launch.”

Then **stop talking**. Let them answer.

If silence:  
> “Most shops do the **[bundle/add-on]** so Google and the call button work together. Want me to hold that price through Friday?”

---

## Don’t do this

- Don’t say “I usually charge $3,000 but for you…” (sounds fake)  
- Don’t go hourly on the first sale  
- Don’t include free unlimited revisions  
- Don’t start work on a handshake with no deposit
